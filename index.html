About Me!

My name is Bobby Paley and I want to gain as many experiences in life as I can as I great believer in living life to the fullest. Once I set my mind to something it is hard to pull me away from it! I'm hardworking and really just want to give back!

I feel this trip will not only be amazing when I'm there but the fundraising journey will teach me a lot of valuable skills as well. I'm really excited about the prospects of me going to Africa and any donations would be really appreciated!

Where Am I Going?

I will be traveling to Tanzania just below Kenya in africa.

What Are We Doing?

While in Kenya, I will be getting a lot of experiences most children my age wouldn't get. Things like hiking around the country and scuba diving to the depths. But most importantly aside from gaining skills for myself I will also be making an impact of peoples lives. As part of the trip we have the privledge of seeing first hand some africa tribes and get to help and experience the way they live!
